\subsection{Missal options for weekdays through December 16}~\label{sect:feriis-ad-16-dec}
    \emph{The Graduale Romanum, before December 16, prescribes the chants from the preceding Sunday.
    However, the Missal gives the following texts for the Entrance and Communion antiphons for
    weekdays. Chants are provided where such exist; otherwise only the texts are given.}

    \filbreak
    \paragraph{Monday}
    ENTRANCE ANTIPHON           Cf. Jer 31: 10; Is 35: 4\\
    \emph{Audite verbum Domini, gentes, et annuntiate illud in finibus terrae: Ecce Salvator noster adveniet, et iam nolite timere.}\\
    Hear the word of the Lord, O nations;
    declare it to the distant lands:
    Behold, our Savior will come; you need no longer fear.

    \textbf{Communion:}
    \gregorioscore{Antiphona/veni-domine-visitare/missal.gabc}


    \filbreak
    \paragraph{Tuesday}
    \textbf{Entrance antiphon:} Ecce Dominus veniet (\emph{comm.}),~\pageref{co:ecce-dominus-veniet}.

    COMMUNION ANTIPHON           Cf. 2 Tm 4: 8\\
    \emph{Coronam iustitiae reddet iustus iudex iis qui diligunt adventum eius.}\\
    The Just Judge will bestow a crown of righteousness
    on those who eagerly await his coming.


    \filbreak
    \paragraph{Wednesday}
    ENTRANCE ANTIPHON           Cf. Hb 2: 3; 1 Cor 4: 5\\
    \emph{Veniet Dominus et non tardabit, et illuminabit abscondita tenebrarum, et manifestabit se ad omnes gentes.}\\
    The Lord will come and he will not delay.
    He will illumine what is hidden in darkness
    and reveal himself to all the nations.

    COMMUNION ANTIPHON           Cf. Is 40: 10; 35: 5\\
    \emph{Ecce Dominus noster cum virtute veniet, ut illuminet oculos servorum suorum.}\\
    Behold, our Lord will come with power
    and will enlighten the eyes of his servants


    \filbreak
    \paragraph{Thursday}~\\
    \textbf{Entrance antiphon:} Prope es tu,~\pageref{in:prope-es-tu}.\\

    COMMUNION ANTIPHON           Ti 2: 12-13\\
    \emph{Iuste et pie vivamus in hoc saeculo, exspectantes beatam spem et adventum gloriae magni Dei.}\\
    Let us live justly and devoutly in this age,
    as we await the blessed hope
    and the coming of the glory of our great God.


    \filbreak
    \paragraph{Friday}
    ENTRANCE ANTIPHON          \\
    \emph{Ecce Dominus veniet cum splendore descendens visitare populum suum in pace, et constituere super eum vitam sempiternam.}\\
    Behold, the Lord will come
    descending with splendor to visit his people with peace,
    and he will bestow on them eternal life.

    COMMUNION ANTIPHON           Cf. Phil 3: 20-21\\
    \emph{Salvatorem exspectamus Dominum Iesum Christum, qui reformabit corpus humilitatis nostrae, configuratum corpori claritatis suae.}\\
    We await a savior, the Lord Jesus Christ,
    who will change our mortal bodies,
    to conform with his glorified body.


    \filbreak
    \paragraph{Saturday}
    ENTRANCE ANTIPHON           Cf. Ps 80 (79): 4, 2\\
    \emph{Veni, et ostende nobis faciem tuam, Domine, qui sedes super Cherubim, et salvi erimus.}\\
    Come and show us your face, O Lord,
    who are seated upon the Cherubim, and we will be saved.

    \textbf{Communion:}
    \gregorioscore{Antiphona/ecce-venio-cito/missal.gabc}
