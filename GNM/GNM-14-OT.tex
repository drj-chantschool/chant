\subsection{14th Week in Ordinary Time}

\paragraph{Entrance Antiphon \& Introit} \emph{Cf. Ps 48 (47): 10-11}
Your merciful love, O God,
we have received in the midst of your temple.
Your praise, O God,
like your name,
reaches the end of the earth;
your right hand is filled with saving justice.

   \small

   let me never be put to shame.

   and grant us your salvation. 
   \Vbar O Lord, you have favored your land, 
   and brought back the captives of Jacob.
