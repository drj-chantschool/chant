% To the Lord in the hour of my distress *\\
% I call and he answers me.\\
``O Lord, save my soul from \textbf{ly}ing lips, *\\
from the tongue of the \emph{de}\textbf{ceit}ful.''

What shall he pay you in \textbf{re}turn, *\\
O \emph{trea}\textbf{cherous} tongue?\\
The warrior's arrows \textbf{shar}pened *\\
and coals, red-\emph{hot}, \textbf{blaz}ing.

Alas, that I abide a stranger in \textbf{Mesh}ech, *\\
dwell among the tents \emph{of} \textbf{Ke}dar!

Long enough have I been \textbf{dwel}ling *\\
with those \emph{who} \textbf{hate} peace.\\
I am for peace, but when \textbf{I} speak, *\\
they are \emph{for} \textbf{fight}ing.
