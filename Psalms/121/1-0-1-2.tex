% I lift up my eyes to the mountains: *\\
% from where shall come my help?\\
My help shall come from \textbf{the} Lord *\\
who made \emph{hea}ven \textbf{and} earth.

May he never allow you to \textbf{stum}ble! *\\
Let him \emph{sleep} not, \textbf{your} guard.\\
No, he sleeps not nor \textbf{slum}bers, *\\
\emph{Is}-ra-\textbf{el's} guard.

The Lord is your guard and \textbf{your} shade; *\\
at your \emph{right} side \textbf{he} stands.\\
By day the sun shall not \textbf{smite} you *\\
nor the \emph{moon} in \textbf{the} night.

The Lord will guard you from \textbf{e}vil, *\\
he \emph{will} guard \textbf{your} soul.\\
The Lord will guard your going and \textbf{com}ing *\\
both now \emph{and} for \textbf{e}ver.
